Game Rules: Toads and Frogs

  • Players: 2 (or 1 vs Computer)
  • Objective: Move all your pieces forward and block your opponent.
  • Toads start on the left and move right.
  • Frogs start on the right and move left.
  • You can move to an adjacent empty cell or jump over an opponent.
  • No double jumps allowed.
  • Toads go first.
